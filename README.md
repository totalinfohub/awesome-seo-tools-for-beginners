### Keyword Research

- **Google Keyword Planner** - A free tool from Google to discover new keywords. Requires a Google Ads account.
- **Ubersuggest** - A freemium tool by Neil Patel that offers keyword ideas, content ideas, and backlink data.
- **Ahrefs Free Keyword Generator** - A great free tool to find keyword ideas for Google, Bing, YouTube, and Amazon.
- **AnswerThePublic** - Visualizes search questions and suggested autocomplete searches.

### On-Page SEO

- **Rank Math SEO** - A powerful WordPress plugin to optimize your content. Offers a content analysis tool.
- **Yoast SEO** - The most popular WordPress SEO plugin for on-page optimization.
- **Surfer SEO** - A content intelligence tool that helps you write perfectly optimized content. (Paid)

### Technical SEO

- **Google Search Console** - An essential free tool from Google to monitor your site's health, indexing status, and performance.
- **Screaming Frog SEO Spider** - A powerful desktop program that crawls your website to find technical SEO issues. (Free version is limited)
- **GTmetrix** - A free tool to analyze your website's speed and performance.

### Link Building

- **Ahrefs Backlink Checker** - A free tool to see the top 100 backlinks to any website or URL.
- **Hunter.io** - Helps you find email addresses for outreach campaigns. (Freemium)

### Rank Tracking

- **SERProbot** - A simple and free Google rank checker.
- **Ahrefs Rank Tracker** - A reliable rank tracker that is part of their paid suite.

### WordPress SEO Plugins

- **Rank Math** - A feature-rich SEO plugin with a clean interface.
- **Yoast SEO** - The classic and most widely used SEO plugin for WordPress.
- **All in One SEO Pack** - Another popular alternative to Yoast and Rank Math.
